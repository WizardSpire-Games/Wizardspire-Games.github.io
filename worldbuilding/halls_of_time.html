The Halls of Time

ACTION: Into Tharbanum

SCENE: outdoors, forest, edge of elven town during an Ork attack. Elves stole artifact from the orcs (enchanted sword)
HISTORY - the elves believe the sword is a magical key to an ancient city and the knowledge contained within. The city
has been lost for for a milenium (an age) and the elves wanted to recover the sword before the evil overlord who commands
the orcs figures out what they have found. Now the elves believe they need to find the lost map of Angkor Throm to open
the seal to the Halls of Time and the knowledge it contains. What the elves don't know is that there is a third artifact
that must be put together with the key and the map to unlock the secrets of the beginning of time and the end of days.
SCENE: (PAN ACROSS THE 7 LANDS OF THE WORLD AS THE NARRATOR SPEAKS) The quest begins with a journey to consult the ancient
tomb of knowledge held by the magical elite within the halls of time in the great wastes hidden from all but the greatest
heroes is the Wizard Spire. This great fortress is guarded by the greatest wizards of the age and hidden away to protect
the knowledge contained within from the prying eyes of evil's minions.
SCENE: (OPENING with view from the town overlooking the fields where a massive army of orcs, goblins, trolls, and ogres
form up around the city blocking every entrance and exit.)
SCENE: (in a large room with ornate carvings of vines and leaves in smoke blackened marble long pews fill the center of
the room facing a dias of silver inlaid marble.) an old wizened elf chastizes a youngelf for his use of dark magic.
Finally offereing an opportunity to attone for his transgression by carrying the key (sword) to the Wizard Spire to find
the secret resting place of the map of ages and unlock the halls of knowledge in the ancient city of Angkor Throm.
SCENE: accepting the key and the quest the hero flees through the secret tunnel under the city.
(fade to black)
